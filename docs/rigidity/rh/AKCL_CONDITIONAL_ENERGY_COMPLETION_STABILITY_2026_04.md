# AKCL Conditional Energy Completion Stability (2026-04)

## Status
Conditional.

## Standing model assumption
Use
\[
\mathcal A_{\mathrm{AKCL}}=\mathbb R^m,
\qquad
E[a]=a^\top M_E a,
\qquad
\|a\|_{\mathcal A_{\mathrm{AKCL}}}=\|a\|_2.
\]

## Exact stability statement
If
\[
a_n\to a
\quad\text{in}\quad
\|\cdot\|_{\mathcal A_{\mathrm{AKCL}}},
\]
then
\[
E[a_n]\to E[a].
\]

## Exact reason
The map
\[
a\longmapsto a^\top M_E a
\]
is a continuous quadratic form on the finite-dimensional normed space
\[
\mathbb R^m.
\]

## Finish condition
This stability statement is exact only under the standing model assumption above.
